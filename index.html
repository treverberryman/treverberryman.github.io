Trever Berryman


✨🕹GAME DESIGNER + DEVELOPER🏳️‍🌈✨

Interdisciplinarian & Mindfulness Practitioner

Hi my name is Trever! My focus is in game design and serious games. I'm all about iterative play test driven design.

I'm currently working on Deep Space Donuts, a queer dating simulator that's mixed with an arcade shooter with the help of my team at Double Dog Games!

My lifelong mission is to make a difference in the world through the use of
video games as a creative medium for change. To do this, I will leverage design to make these types of games attain mainstream success.

Feel free to contact me with the provided links below!

Deep Space Donuts

Game Designer / Producer



• Deep Space Donuts is both a ✨🏳️‍🌈 queer dating simulator as well as an arcade shooter 🚀✨.

IXS Odyssey (VR)

Game Designer / Producer


This was my Senior Capstone Project. IXS Odyssey is a VR space-themed sci-fi survival horror title that was actively in development at Chimerical Collective. I co-founded Chimerical with Christopher McCardle.

• Helped with game design, level design, producing, developing, and managing.
• Designed a VR survival horror game prototype made in Unity3D on the HTC Vive for a college capstone project.
• Managed documentation while tuning and iterating based on player feedback from external / internal play tests.
• Worked on level implementation and pacing with mechanics such as the headlamp battery drain and hand recharging pads.

The Exception

Game Designer



• Pyromance is an entry for the Emotions and Feelings Jam with the theme of "togetherness".
• I made The Exception for the secret event in 2019 called #meditationgames. Meditations is a launcher that, every day, loads a small game and an accompanying text as a meditation, distraction, lesson, or inspiration for that day.
• My meditation was about being queer and living in the southern United States. A short experience made in Twine.

Pyromance

Game Designer



• Pyromance is an entry for the Emotions and Feelings Jam with the theme of "togetherness".
• I chose to make a game about three polyamourous people sitting at a campfire making choices to make it to the end of the night!
• If you can't balance the trio, the relationship fails and you don't see the sunrise.
• Worked with Dr. Mike Reddy.
