Trever Berryman’s ePortfolio

TECHNICAL GAME DESIGNER, INTERDISCIPLINARIAN, MINDFULNESS PRACTICIONER

I graduated from LSU with my Bachelors in Interdisciplinary Studies (Digital Media Tech, International Studies, IT Management and Sociology) in May 2017. My strongest lifelong passion is game development. More specifically, my passion is to make a difference in the world through the use of video games as a creative medium for change. My focus is in game design, VR development, programming and serious games.

When I first started my BIS degree I thought that the safe route would be to become a consultant for tech companies and that I would be satisfied with that path. The further I got along, the more I realized that video game development was my true passion. The minors I chose needed no modification in order to follow the path of game development and only needed a change in perception.
Feel free to contact me with the provided links below!

(C++)ave Story

I'm currently rewriting the hit game Cave Story from scratch in C++.



IXS Odyssey (VR)


This is a VR space-themed sci-fi survival horror game that's currently in development at Chimerical Collective.

You find yourself waking up in a cryotube after a long sleep. You are the caption of a science bio-research vessel. You were in the cryotube because of an injury sustained during the last mission. Your personal robotic assistant is waiting for you when you wake up. You find that there was a security override that kicked in and brought you out of stasis. Immediately you hear someone over your comm system trying to contact someone. What the Captain Blake doesn’t know is that a mysterious monster lurks in the depths of the ship. Along the way you discover supplies, survivors, and learn more about the person deep in the ship calling out for help.

Full Spectrum Witch

Play as a powerful chromatic witch trying to rescue her sister from being burned at the stake! Pull color directly from the environment around you to power your abilities and combat those that stand between you and your sister. Race against the clock or you’ll both be desaturated! A link to the GGJ17 page can be found here. This game won Best Feature at the LSU jam site in Baton Rouge, LA.

Block.Flock

This website was created as a team project for a Object Oriented Design course using Truffle, IPFS, and the Ethereum network. It stores your files in IPFS as a hash then you can send that hash over the ethereum blockchain in exchange for ether. Our latest build is hosted in GitHub which you can view here.
