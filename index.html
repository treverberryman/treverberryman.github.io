Trever Berryman’s ePortfolio

GAME PROGRAMMER, INTERDISCIPLINARIAN, MINDFULNESS PRACTICIONER

My strongest lifelong passion is game development programming. More specifically, my passion is to make a difference in the world through combining video games, social analytics, and civic technology. This combination comes from my ability to synthesize disciplines from various fields in my experience as an interdisciplinarian. Mindfulness meditation has become a huge component of how I operate and its effects pervade through everything I do.

When I first started my BIS degree I thought that the safe route would be to become a consultant for tech companies and that I would be satisfied with that path. The further I got along the more I realized that video game development was my true passion. The minors I chose needed no modification in order to follow the path of game development and only needed a change in perception.
