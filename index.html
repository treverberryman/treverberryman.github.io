GAME DESIGNER + DEVELOPER


Interdisciplinarian & Mindfulness Practicioner

I've made it out of college and I'm out in the world finding my own in the games industry. My lifelong plan is to make a difference in the world through the use of video games as a creative medium for change and to leverage design to make these types of games attain mainstream success. Serious games have the right idea but are nowhere near mainstream. I think there a wonderful oppurtunity here.

My focus is in game design, VR development, programming and serious games.
