The BIS Degree & My Professional Persona

Course Trajectory Assessments:

I’ve taken similar tests before on other websites such as the Myers-Briggs evaluation. I think this assessment inventory pointed me in the direction I was already heading towards and confirmed some qualities about myself. I did learn a few details about being a game developer that I did not know before.

Personality Inventory:

Well, I was surprised to find that this time I had the ENFJ personality type instead of ENFP. According to TypeFocus my personality type does not fit well with video game development, which is what I want to do. I did a little research online and my personality type has apparently been known to still mesh well in the gaming industry. I think this exercise helped me understand that I need to express my artistic and enterprising sides more. This hasn’t changed my plans for post-graduation and has only strengthened them.
