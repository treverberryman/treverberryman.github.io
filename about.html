About Me

Profile Summary
Quick learner, Motivated, Hard worker, Multi-tasker, Positive, Friendly, Creative, and Great personality.

My name is Sarah Baldwin and I am currently an undergraduate at Louisiana State University majoring in Computer Science with a concentration in Software Engineering and minors in Mathematics and Information Technology Management.

As a student, I am interested in Software advancements and research, Human Computer Interaction (HCI), User Interface (UI)/Tangible User Interface (TUI)/Graphical User Interface (GUI) Design, and Front-end (& some Back-end) on Web/Mobile Sites and Applications. From contemporary issues and their policies to the sciences behind them all, my fascination with the world never ends. Because of my incessant need to discover and experience new things every day, I have developed an interminable desire to learn.

Life changes from day to day and, as a result, my experiences do too. Be it from the people I meet or from the things I read, I feel there is never enough time to learn it all.

I have fun with almost everything I do and, as a result, I like to experience new adventures. From web design, graphics, creative writing (working on a novel), and photography at home to farming and livestock and other outdoor activities, the things I enjoy vary greatly.
